May 14, 2023 · Brunch Club

SAÏA Brunch Club at The Nest with Pilates by Riya

A unique morning filled with community and connection, high above the city.

If you are new here, welcome! The SAÏA Brunch Club is a monthly meet-up that features wellness, Pilates and delicious food. This gathering is the perfect way to celebrate sisterhood, learn from each other, and create meaningful connections with amazing women.

This edition we took over the beautiful rooftop at The Nest in Treehouse Hotel, with a panoramic view of London's skyline. The breathtaking views were the perfect complement to our morning of Pilates and our hearty brunch.

Our instructor

Pilates by Riya

Riya's journey with Pilates started as a way to find joy and confidence in her own body, after struggling with body issues for most of her life. After ten years of self-practice, she decided to become a teacher herself — to create a session that serves the body without breaking it down.

She strives to help her clients grow in confidence and strength, and led us through the transformative power of Pilates with the city stretched out below.

The menu

Mexican cuisine that pairs bold flavours with organic ingredients at Madera. This brunch came with an exciting twist as we introduced the traditional Mexican brunch dish, Huevos a la Mexicana — plus your favourite brunch staples, from coconut parfait to avo toast and freshly baked pastries.

A delicious selection to choose from as you took in the breathtaking panoramic views of London.

As always, the SAÏA Brunch Club is designed so that every guest gets the opportunity to network and connect over a beautifully prepared brunch.
