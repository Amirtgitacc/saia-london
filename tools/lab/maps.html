NW3 map directions.

Four ways to draw "NW3 & Hampstead" for the Pilates band, all on the SAÏA palette (cream, ink, terracotta · Playfair + Inter). Each draws itself in once on view. Pick one and it gets ported into home.html Flow Level 3. This page never ships to Pages.

01   Ink line-art

Editorial, calm. Monoline ink with a faint terracotta Heath. Roads draw in then the pin lands. Reads like a magazine map.

02   Tube-map

Confident, wayfinding. Hampstead (NW3) as a white interchange, with Belsize Park and Swiss Cottage nearby. Straight 45°/90° lines. Says "easy to get to."

03   Topographic

Soft, cartographic. Elevation-style contours breathing out from Hampstead's hill. The most "map" of the four, very textural.

04   Hand-drawn

Warm, made-by-hand. Wobbled double strokes and a scribbled Heath that matches the watercolour figures. The pin gently bobs.
